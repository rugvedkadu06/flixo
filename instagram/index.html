How Instagram Reels Editing Works?

 

Let’s delve into the specifics. First of all the maximum length for a Reel in 2023 is 90 seconds (whereas an Instagram story is 60 seconds) and they must maintain a 9:16 aspect ratio. These creative challenges are turned into advantages by a talented Instagram reels editor. Videos are both filmed and edited with a vertical alignment.

Your awesome content + a great hook + our Intsa Reels Editing Services = more engagement and audience growth.

Creating a scroll stopping Reel involves a few steps:

Footage Selection:

Choose the best clips that align with the story or message you want to convey. A reel should start with a strong hook so the user is forced to stop scrolling. (Tip: You can even re-purpose content from your long-form videos).

Trimming & Sequencing:

Cut down clips to fit the desired length and sequence them so your message is concise, entertaining, while still highlighting your key-points. We’re experts at cutting out the fluff!
Transitions, Zooms, Pans: Smooth transitions along with pans and zooms can make a Reel more engaging, ensuring viewers stay hooked from start to finish. A video that holds attention should not be too static or overuse the same elements.

Colour correction/stabilization:

Sometimes the raw footage we’re sent can be vastly improved by optimising the colour balance and removing jerky movements.

Music & Sound:

Add background music or foley sound effects. Ensure they align with the mood and actually enhance the content.

Text, Graphics & Thumbnails:

Enhance your Reel with on-screen text, gifs/animations, emojis, suitable thumbnails and other graphic elements to emphasize key points or add creative flair. It’s well studied that visual metaphors and images can improve retention and understanding of a subject.

Tapping into trends:

To stay relevant and entertaining make use of trending content ideas, viral memes and trending music. This is a great way to get the algorithm on your side to reach a larger audience.
